Why do you need a documentation tool?

DOCUMENTS
ALL OVER THE PLACE

Processes are often spread across different tools and assets.
Shared docs lost in limbo, old app accounts, physical notes in the
bin or even incomplete flowcharts can be a headache.
Processes
Matter

It can be challenging to make sure that your team is on the same page.
Helping your team perform essential business operations
translates into improved productivity and business efficiency.

Small
Businesses
Evolve Fast

There are ton of new wiki, help-center, flowcharting and productivity tools.
While they serve a purpose they are a far from ideal solution
to keep organizational documents updated.

How are we solving this?

We love flowcharts because they give us the visual,
and lets us understand a process at a glance.
We love docs because they give us the much
needed detail to follow throughwith actionable steps.
