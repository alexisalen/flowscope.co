All you need in one place


Flowscope keeps all your words and diagrams together. It's the one source of truth. Add templates,attachments, media, checklists or any other action items associated with your business processes.

A view for everyone


Flowscope allows managers to add team members to different work and process areas creating transparent collaborative spaces where procedures live.

Grows and adapts with you


Flowscope allows you to easily update thecompany's processes as you grow and scale. Changes will be with the team in seconds.
